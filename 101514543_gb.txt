George Brown College Overview

George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Canada. 
It offers diploma, advanced diploma, and postgraduate programs in 
technology, business, health sciences, hospitality, and more.

The college focuses on hands-on learning and industry-relevant skills. 
Many programs include practical labs, real-world projects, and 
co-op opportunities that prepare students for employment.

As a student in the Computer Systems Technician program, 
I gain practical experience in networking, Linux, Windows Server, 
virtualization, and cloud technologies.

George Brown emphasizes applied education, teamwork, 
and career readiness.
